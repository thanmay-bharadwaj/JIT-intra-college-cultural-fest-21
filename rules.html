RULES

Standard Rules

* Date: 13-08-2021
* Platform : Zoom / Google meet.
* Make sure internet connection is good and there are no disturbances
during the meet or else it might lead to disqualification.
* No registration fee will be charged.
* Winners prize amount ₹1,000.
* Runners prize amount ₹ 500.

WAR OF WORDS

RULES:
* This is a solo event.
* Event takes place in 3 rounds.
* Topic for the first round will be given 10 minutes prior to the event individually.
* The participants will be given a time limit of 2+1 minutes for each round.
* In the first round the participants are required to introduce themselves and state their arguments regarding the topic.
* Based on their performance, participants will be selected for second round.
* Topic for second round will be given 10 minutes prior to the round.
* Topic and Opponents will be chosen by the judge.
* Based on toss, participants will be allowed to speak "for or against" the topic for 2+1 minutes.
* According to their performance, participants will be selected for third round.
* The third round will be a rebuttal round. Every participant can rebut their competitors for 2+1 minutes.
* In third round participants can choose to rebut their opponents or present their arguments and give a conclusion for their argument.

MESMERIZE

RULES:
* This is a solo event.
* Event is divided into 2 categories Western and Classical.
* Karoke is allowed for Western only.
* Participant can have one instrument to accompany them.
* Evaluation will be done based on singing performance only.
* Event takes place in 2 rounds:
• In preliminary round, registered participants have to send a 3 minutes pre-recorded video through google drive link and mail it to: virtualcultfest21@jyothyit.ac.in with your name, event name and genre name mentioned in the mail on or before 9th August. The selected participants for final round will receive a confirmation email on August 11th.
• In final round, selected participants will have to perform LIVE in their genre for 5+1 minutes.

MAKE US BELIEVE

RULES:
* This is a group event.
* This is a LIVE event.
* Each group must consist of minimum 3 and maximum of 4 participants.
* Skit must be performed in English, Kannada or Hindi.
* Duration is 10-12 minutes.
* Topic for the skit can be chosen by the participants.
* Usage of foul language, promotion of drugs, gender discrimination, religion discrimination, sexual content, mockery about universities/famous figures, anti-national lines and other sensitive issues will not be entertained and may lead to disqualification.

INSTRUMENT

RULES:
* This is a solo event.
* Each participant is allowed to play any instrument of his/her choice.
* Duration is 5+1 minutes.
* Karaoke is not allowed.
* As this is LIVE event no pre-recorded audio or video clippings are entertained.

ONE MAN SHOW

RULES:
* This is an individual event.
* Event takes place in two rounds:
• In preliminary round, registered participants have to send a 3 minutes pre-recorded video through google drive link mail it to:virtualcultfest21@jyothyit.ac.in on or before 9th August. The selected participants for final round will receive a confirmation email on August 11th.
